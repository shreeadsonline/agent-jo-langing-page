Faq's

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why choose JAIN Online?
JAIN Online offers UGC-entitled degrees from JAIN (Deemed-to-be University), a NAAC A++ accredited institution. We combine academic excellence with industry-relevant micro-credentials and 100% placement assistance to ensure our learners are future-ready.
2. What is the mode of learning?
The programs are delivered 100% online through our advanced Learning Management System (LMS). This features live interactive sessions, recorded lectures, digital course materials, and collaborative discussion forums.
3. How many credits does the program have?
Our degree programs follow UGC guidelines for credit distribution. Typically, our MBA and MCA programs comprise between 80 to 100+ credits spread across four semesters.
4. How is an online degree offered through JAIN Online different from correspondence, open or distance learning modes?

An online degree offered through JAIN Online is delivered through a structured digital ecosystem that includes live classes, multimedia learning resources, continuous assessments, and regular learner and faculty interaction via a Learning Management System. This model enables guided learning, peer collaboration, and real-time academic support, creating an interactive and technology-driven learning experience.

Distance education, on the other hand, relies largely on self-study using printed or static digital material with limited real-time engagement and academic support. A key distinction also lies in the examination process - online programs conduct proctored examinations using secure technology-enabled monitoring, while distance education typically requires learners to appear for center-based examinations at authorized venues under in person invigilation.

5. What documents are required for admission?
You will generally need scanned copies of your 10th and 12th mark sheets, graduation certificates (for PG programs), a valid government-issued ID proof (Aadhaar, PAN, or Passport), and recent passport-sized photographs.
6. Is this online degree accepted by employers in India and abroad?
Yes, UGC-entitled online degrees are fully recognized by government and private sector employers worldwide for recruitment, promotions, and eligibility for competitive examinations.
7. Can I use this degree for higher studies (PhD, MPhil, studies abroad)?
Absolutely. An online degree from JAIN Online is legally equivalent to any on-campus degree, making you eligible to pursue PhDs, MPhils, or higher education at prestigious international universities.
8. What happens if I miss a live class?
No worries. All live classes are recorded and uploaded to the Learning Management System within 24 hours. You can revisit these recordings as many times as you need during your program.
9. How are exams conducted and proctored?
Exams are conducted online using an advanced AI-proctored system. This allows you to take your exams safely from home while maintaining the highest levels of integrity through webcam monitoring and browser security.
10. Is there technical support if I face platform issues?
Yes, we provide robust technical support. You can reach out to our helpdesk via email, phone, or live chat within the LMS for any assistance related to platform access or connectivity.
11. How interactive is the LMS experience?
Our LMS is designed to be highly engaging. It includes features like interactive quizzes, peer-to-peer discussion boards, gamified learning elements, and real-time feedback from instructors.
12. Will I receive faculty mentoring?
Yes, every learner is assigned a dedicated faculty mentor and a "Batch Owner" who provides guidance on academic progress, career planning, and elective selection.
13. How can I get my qualifications evaluated for international immigration?
JAIN Online degrees are recognized by WES (World Education Services) and other global evaluation bodies, facilitating easy credit transfers and qualification recognition for immigration to countries like the USA and Canada.
14. What are Micro-Credential Courses (MCCs)?
MCCs are short, specialized courses focused on high-demand skills (like AI, Fintech, or Data Science). They are integrated into your degree to provide practical, industry-specific expertise that makes you stand out to employers.
15. Who delivers the Micro-Credential Courses?
MCCs are delivered by top industry practitioners and global experts from world-leading organizations, ensuring you gain insights that are directly applicable in the modern workplace.
